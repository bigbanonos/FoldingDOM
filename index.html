NYC Joseph

Music Art

I will
stroll with—
you, and

I will
bowl with—
you, too.

The light of a candle
Is transferred to another candle—
Spring twilight

I write, erase, rewrite
Erase again, and then
A poppy blooms.

Over the wintry
Forest, winds howl in rage
With no leaves to blow.

The summer grasses.
All that remains
Of warriors’ dreams.
